Frame Pack

Client asked for a bag to fit on frame. Ok. I asked what the bag was to hold and he informed me that it would need to hold at least one quarter Elk. Uhhhh... How big is that? He replied, just make it as big as you can. So I made it as big as I could with respect to the frame.
